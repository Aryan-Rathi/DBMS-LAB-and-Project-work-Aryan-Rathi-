Experiment 2: Data Retrieval using SQL
Problem Statement
The objective of this experiment is to write and execute SQL queries to retrieve specific data from the EMPLOYEE and DEPARTMENT tables based on various conditions.

Queries to Implement:
1. List all distinct jobs in the Employee table.

2. List all information about employees in Department Number 30.

3. Find all department numbers with department names greater than 20.

4. Find all information about all managers and clerks in Department 30.

5. List the Employee name, Employee numbers, and department of all clerks.

6. Find all managers not in Department 30.

7. List information about all employees in Department 10 who are not managers or clerks.

8. Find employees and jobs earning between 1200 and 1400.

9. List the Name and Department Number of employees who are clerks, analysts, or salesmen.

10. List the Name and Department Number of employees whose names begin with 'M'.
