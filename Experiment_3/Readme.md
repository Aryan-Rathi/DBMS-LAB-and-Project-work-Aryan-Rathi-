Experiment 3: Advanced Data Retrieval and Pattern Matching
Problem Statement
The objective of this experiment is to perform advanced data filtering using sorting, string patterns, and computed columns.

Queries to Implement:
1. List all employees and jobs in Department 30 in descending order by salary.

2. List job and Department Number of employees whose name are five letters long, begin with "A", and end with "N".

3. Display the name of employees whose name starts with the alphabet 'S'.

4. Display the names of employees whose name ends with the alphabet 'S'.

5. Display the names of employees working in department number 10, 20, or 40, or employees working as clerks, salesmen, or analysts.

6. Display employee number and names for employees who earn a commission.

7. Display employee number and total salary for each employee.

8. Display employee number and annual salary for each employee.

9. Display names of all employees working as clerks and drawing a salary more than 3,000.

10. Display names of employees working as clerk, salesman, or analyst and drawing a salary more than 3,000.
